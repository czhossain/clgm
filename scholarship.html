Eligibility & Terms

  • Open to Malaysian students only.
  • Applicable for the first semester of study only.
  • Academic achievement and co-curricular involvement will be considered.
  • Continuation beyond first semester (if any) is subject to academic performance and the terms set by KLUST.

How to Apply

  1. Apply to your chosen programme at KLUST.
  2. Prepare the documents listed below.
  3. Submit your scholarship application together with your programme admission documents.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts/certificates
  • Identification (NRIC/Passport)
  • Co-curricular certificates (if applicable)
  • Other supporting documents
