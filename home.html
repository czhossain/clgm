WHAT OUR STUDENTS SAY

Excellent Academic Experience

The main reason I enrolled in KLUST was because of the scholarship offered through the Ministry of Higher Education. I still enjoy the tuition fee waiver throughout my semester. The facilities on campus are excellent, with additional discussion rooms and a new library that will be completed soon. I am very much looking forward to continuing my studies here.

Engineering Excellence at KLUST

I enrolled in the Diploma in Civil Engineering Technology and continued with the Degree Programme in Civil Engineering. Studying at the Faculty of Civil Engineering has been enjoyable and has greatly enhanced my engineering skills. I am doing well academically and personally enjoy being involved in sports activities. My experience at KLUST has been exceptional.

Outstanding Architecture Program

My experience as an Architecture student at KLUST has been tremendously impactful. I have learned so much about architecture and take note of all the knowledge and experience gained. The expertise of our lecturers has been instrumental in expanding my interest and skills in this field.
