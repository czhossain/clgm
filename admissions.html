Intakes

There are three intakes per year. All programmes are offered in March, June and September / October intakes.

International students are required to submit applications two months before semester begins.

March Intake

Application Deadline: January

June Intake

Application Deadline: April

Application Deadline: July

Entry Requirements

Malaysian Students

  • SPM with minimum 5 credits including Bahasa Malaysia and English
  • STPM with minimum 2 principals
  • Diploma from recognized institutions
  • Foundation studies
View Application Forms

International Students

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • English proficiency requirement
  • Valid passport
  • Medical check-up report
View Application Forms
