Getting Here

  • By KTM Komuter: Stop at KTM Serdang. Use KLUST Shuttle to campus.
  • By MRT: Stop at MRT Serdang Jaya. Use KLUST Shuttle to campus.
  • By Car: Set destination to "Infrastructure University Kuala Lumpur". Visitor parking available at designated areas.

Shuttle bus operates on weekdays and is not available on public holidays. See the Shuttle Bus Schedule for details.
